2x2 Rubik's Cube Solver

Before you start: find the corner that has yellow + blue + orange stickers — that is your Down-Back-Left (DBL) reference corner.
  1. Rotate the whole cube until that corner sits at the bottom-back-left, with yellow facing down, blue facing back, and orange facing left. Do not move it again.
  2. Tab between facelets and press W R G Y O B to paint — or click a facelet after selecting a palette color.
  3. Fill all 24 stickers (4 per color), then press Solve.
  4. Use Next / Prev to step through the solution one move at a time. Keep the cube in the same orientation throughout.
